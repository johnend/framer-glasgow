Framer Scotland is a monthly meetup for like-minded individuals to come together, talk about their experience with Framer.js and Framer Studio, learn something new and have a good time doing it.

We will be meeting regularly on the first Thursday of every month, beginning in October, at a venue which is as yet a mystery. If you're interested in coming along, whether you are a seasoned prototyping professional, or a newbie looking to level up your skills fill in some information at the bottom of this page and we will be in touch.
