Framer Scotland is a monthly meetup for like-minded individuals to come together, talk about their experience with Framer.js and Framer Studio, learn something new and have a good time doing it.

We will be meeting regularly on the first Thursday of every month at a venue which is as yet a mystery. If you're interested in coming along, whether you are a seasoned prototyping professional, or a newbie looking to level up your skills, just click one of the buttons above and make sure you're always in the loop! Once we have enough interested folks (not many, but ideally more than one!) we will send out a newsletter and various social media posts to let everyone know when the first session will be.
